MBBS in the Philippines - A Top Study Destination

The Philippines offers a unique opportunity for Indian students to pursue an MBBS (MD) degree with an American-based education system. With NMC-approved universities, affordable tuition, and a high success rate in FMGE/NEXT, it’s a top choice for medical education abroad.

All universities follow the BS + MD program (5.5 years) as per NMC guidelines, with 100% English-medium instruction and excellent clinical exposure, making it an ideal destination for international students.

Quick Facts About the Philippines

Capital: Manila
Official Languages: Filipino, English
English Speaking Population: Over 90%
Currency: Philippine Peso (PHP)
Population: 11.5 crores (2023)
Time Difference: 2 hours 30 minutes behind India
Total Land Area: 300,000 km²
Climate: Tropical, 24°C to 32°C

Why Study MBBS in the Philippines for Indian Students in 2025-26?

American-Based Education System: Modeled after the US medical curriculum.
High Success Rate in FMGE/NEXT: Proven track record for Indian students.
100% English-Medium Instruction: No language barrier.
No Entrance Exams (Except NEET): Simplified admission process.
Clinical Exposure: Training in top hospitals.
Affordable Living and Tuition Costs: Cost-effective education.
Indian Food & Hostels Available: Comfort for Indian students.
Safety: Safe environment with student support systems.
Recognition: Degrees recognized by NMC, WHO, ECFMG, FAIMER.

Estimated Living Cost: Hostel & Food: ₹10,000 – ₹15,000/month (Total yearly: ₹1.2 – ₹1.8 lakhs)

Note: NMAT required after BS for MD program entry.
