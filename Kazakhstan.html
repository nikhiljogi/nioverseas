MBBS in Kazakhstan - A Top Study Destination

Located in Central Asia, Kazakhstan is an emerging hub for medical education, attracting students globally with its affordable MBBS programs starting at $3,500 per year. With over 15 medical universities, Kazakhstan offers quality education recognized by international bodies like WHO and NMC.

Kazakhstan’s medical universities provide English-medium courses, modern infrastructure, and practical training, making it an excellent choice for Indian and international students. Graduates are eligible to appear for exams like FMGE, USMLE, and PLAB.

Quick Facts About Kazakhstan

Capital: Astana
Official Language: Kazakh, Russian
English Speaking Population: 20%
Currency: Kazakhstani Tenge (KZT)
Population: 1.95 crores (2023)
Time Difference: 30 minutes behind India
Total Land Area: 2.7 million sq.km
Climate: Continental, -40°C to 35°C

Why Study MBBS in Kazakhstan for Indian Students in 2025-26?

Recognized Universities: 15+ NMC and WHO-approved medical universities.
Affordable Fees: Tuition fees starting at INR 15 lakhs for the entire course.
English Medium: Fully English-taught programs available.
Global Recognition: Degrees recognized by NMC, WHO, and ECFMG.

Safe Environment: Student-friendly and secure campuses.
Quality Education: Modern facilities and experienced faculty.
Easy Admission: No entrance exams like NEET required for eligibility.
